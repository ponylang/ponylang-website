# Last Week in Pony

Last Week in Pony (LWIP) is a weekly newsletter published on Sundays. It covers what happened in the Pony world over the previous week: releases, community discussions, interesting projects, and other noteworthy items.

You can read all past issues in the [Last Week in Pony blog category](https://ponylang.io/blog/categories/last-week-in-pony/).

## Submitting Content

Got something you think should be featured? Add a comment to the [open "Last Week in Pony" issue](https://github.com/ponylang/ponylang-website/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Alast-week-in-pony). There's always one open issue collecting items for the next post. Anything added there will be reviewed for inclusion.

## Writing LWIP

LWIP is written by pulling together items from the open issue, checking for new releases across ponylang repositories, and reviewing activity on Zulip and GitHub. The post is submitted as a PR to the [ponylang-website](https://github.com/ponylang/ponylang-website) repository.
